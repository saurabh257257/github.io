Our Mission

Enabling Battery Manufacturing at Scale

Arambhika Enablers was founded to bridge the gap between premium-grade battery components and the manufacturers who need them. We focus on traceability, precision, and partnership — ensuring your supply chain is never the weak link.

Our manufacturing unit, Swastik Metal Components, operates in Greater Noida with the capacity to produce custom widths, stamped profiles, and bent configurations to exact specifications.
